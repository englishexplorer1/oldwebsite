Your end-to-end partner for every step.

Markets

Cirtec is at the heart of cutting-edge medical devices trusted the world over to extend and improve the quality of human life. As an all-in-one outsourcing partner, the components we produce are found in devices throughout the healthcare industry.Â 

We serve a wide spectrum of markets:

Neuromodulation

Neuromodulation devices are changing the game for treatment of spinal pain, hearing and vision loss, and an ever-expanding range of illnesses. Our custom-built and ready-to-go options help bring these devices to market quickly and cost effectively.

Learn More

Active Implants

Active implants make it easier for doctors and patients to effectively manage chronic conditions, and we provide the full range of support our clients need to bring these complex Class III devices from ideation to design, production, and distribution.

Learn More

Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs)

VADs and circulatory support devices, once used solely as bridge-to-transplant solutions, are increasingly utilized as destination therapy. This uptake in usage strengthens the need for the high-quality, high-durability devices and components we produce.

Learn More

Cardiac Rhythm Management (CRM)

Custom-built and ready-to-go options for devices ranging from defibrillators to pacemakers, built to both IS-1 and DF-4 standards and balancing robust capabilities with increasingly compact designs.

Learn More

Interventional Cardiology

These catheter-based therapies are designed to diagnose and treat coronary, vascular and structural heart diseases and defects. The devices we produce live up to the highest standards of quality, produced under strict ISO 14644-1 Class 7 and Class 8 certified clean rooms.

Learn More

Structural Heart

Our technical experts collaborate directly with customers to define and scope device performance requirements for these minimally invasive procedures. This design and manufacturing approach allows our customers to focus on the therapy leveraging our experience for delivery systems as well as high quality Nitinol tubing for valve replacement/repair.

Learn More

Neurovascular Stents

Neurovascular stents make intracranial stenosis and brain aneurysm treatments possible. Our experience developing microcatheters, together with our expertise in creating high quality Nitinol tubing makes us an ideal partner for developing new neurovascular stents and delivery devices.

Learn More

Diabetes and Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGM)

Our products make the monitoring of glucose levels easy and accurate. We bring considerable experience with sensors, hermetic welding and assembly, power generation and transfer, and fluid dynamics.

Learn More

Electrophysiology

Our global network of design teams and production facilities allows us to build scalable, price-competitive development and production processes for our clients. We understand the higher bar our clients set for their devices, and we help them exceed those expectations.

Learn More

As a part of the Cirtec family of companies, Vascotube is a worldwide leader in medical tubing. Vacotube drew its first seamless Nitinol tube in 1991. In a given year, Vascotube produces over 80,000 feet of stent-quality Nitinol tubing for implantable devices, pushing the feasibility of dimensions and surface quality to levels not previously reached in the industry.

Visit Vascotube

Now a Cirtec company, NovelCath is an internationally recognized pioneer in the manufacture of catheter delivery systems with over 70 years of combined industry experience on the leadership team. From its 40,000 square foot facility in Minnesota, NovelCath specializes in the design, development and manufacture of minimally invasive interventional delivery systems ranging from Structural Heart to Electrophysiology and Neurovascular applications. NovelCath maintains a 3,200 square foot assembly space with a Class 7 clean room, dedicated prototyping lines, catheter assembly, extrusion, braiding, coiling, laser cutting, Nitinol processing and multiple reflow technologies.

Medical Device Coil Winding

Our custom designed equipment allows us to manufacture with tight pitch control on straight, tapered or multi-pitched coils in all wire forms.

All wire coilings for medical components and assemblies are manufactured under our proprietary process in a Class 100,000 clean room for pristine wire coils. Our tooling expertise allows us to consistently develop new methods to meet or exceed our customers‚Äô tolerance requirements.

What we provide

Standard and custom wire coil winding over mandrel or deflection winding are available in a wide range of wire coiling styles and sizes. We can supply all standard and custom materials including flat and shaped wire for coiled products. Our team can provide for all steps of the process, from wire coiling to wire and coil grinding to laser welding and assembly, soldering and coil termination.

ACCEL+

The Only Neurostimulation Platform previously FDA Approved and CE Marked ready for your next therapy

The ACCEL+ neuromodulation platform is our most complete and comprehensive solution for bringing our clients’ products to market quickly and cleanly. ACCEL+ is manufactured and configured to each project’s specifications, and with it our clients can harness their regulatory strategy by leveraging the Device Master File on record with the FDA, and choose from a full suite of peripherals, leads, and surgical instruments for their project. ACCEL+ is ideal for research, early feasibility studies, and accelerated launch strategies.

Key Features:

  • Two configurations
    • 3 ports x 8 independent channels (3 leads, each with 8-electrodes)
    • 2 ports x 12 independent channels (2 leads, each with 12-electrodes)
  • 24 dedicated current sources (0 to 15 mA per channel – 30mA total output maximum)
  • Enhanced wireless communication capabilities
  • Rechargeable, deep-discharge recovery battery
  • Biphasic Square Pulse Shape
  • Small volume (19.5 and 20.5 cc)Configurable from 2 to 32 channels
